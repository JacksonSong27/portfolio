SPATIALTUTOR

Carle Illinois College of Medicine


Research Assistant
May 2025 – Present

Project: Object-Aware Mixed Reality Training for Procedural Medical Skills Training with AI-Driven Support



Description:

SPATIALTUTOR is an innovative mixed reality training system that provides AI-driven support for procedural medical skills training. The system uses object-aware technology to enhance learning experiences in medical education. By integrating spatial computing with artificial intelligence, SPATIALTUTOR creates immersive training environments where medical students can practice complex procedures with real-time AI guidance and feedback.

The system leverages advanced computer vision and machine learning algorithms to track and understand the spatial relationships between medical tools, anatomical structures, and trainee movements. This object-aware capability allows the AI to provide contextually relevant support, detecting when trainees encounter difficulties and offering adaptive assistance in real-time.


IEEE Publication:

"SPATIALTUTOR: Object-Aware Mixed Reality Training for Procedural Medical Skills Training with AI-Driven Support" - Presented at IEEE AIxVR 2026 (AIxVR 26)

This work was accepted and presented at the IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Extended Reality (AIxVR), showcasing cutting-edge research at the intersection of AI, mixed reality, and medical training.













EmSim

Illinois Police Training Institute


Research Assistant
October 2024 – May 2025

Project: AI-Driven VR Simulation for Scenario-Based De-Escalation Training in Law Enforcement



Description:

EmSim is an AI-driven virtual reality simulation system designed for scenario-based de-escalation training in law enforcement. The system provides realistic, immersive training scenarios to help officers develop critical communication and de-escalation skills in high-stakes situations. Through advanced AI technology, EmSim creates dynamic, responsive training environments where officers can practice de-escalation techniques with virtual characters that adapt their behavior based on the officer's communication style and actions.

The system uses natural language processing and behavioral analysis to evaluate officer performance in real-time, providing immediate feedback and personalized training recommendations. By simulating various high-pressure scenarios, EmSim helps law enforcement personnel build confidence and competence in de-escalation strategies, ultimately contributing to safer interactions between officers and the public.


IEEE Publication:

"EmSim: An AI-Driven VR Simulation for Scenario-Based De-Escalation Training in Law Enforcement" - Presented at IEEE AIxVR 2026 (AIxVR 26)

This research was accepted and presented at the IEEE Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Extended Reality (AIxVR), demonstrating innovative applications of AI and VR technology in law enforcement training.
